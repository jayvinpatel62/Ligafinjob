We at Benchmarx believe that knowledge is the key to success in this fiercely competitive job market. Our training methodology is streamlined not only to provide a detailed coverage of the course content and prepare students to succeed in globally competitive examinations but also train them to practically apply theoretical concepts and be a better fit in the financial industry.

Our USP includes -
  • Healthy mix of professionals with relevant industry experience and strong academic background in CAs, CFAs and MBAs from top B-schools including rank holders as faculty members.
