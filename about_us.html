ABOUT US

The brains behind Vahura and Careersanta have come together to create Legafinjobs to take their passion for recruitment to the next level. It is a unique team of CAs and lawyers with more than a decade of expertise in recruitment along with prior experience working in legal, tax, and finance domains with leading organizations. Our vision is to create an efficient and cost-effective platform to revolutionize the job search and hiring process for the legal and finance community.

legafinjobs.com is India’s first dedicated portal for jobs, internships, coaching and career guidance forniche segment of commerce professionals being CA, CS, CWA, CFA, MBA, B Com, BAF, BMS, BFM, etc. Ourteam comprises of professionals in the same fraternity who are ideally placed to understand the needs ofemployers, candidates, coaching classes and students.

We have been connecting candidates and employers as per their requirements for over 6 years now and were earlier known as Praccum. Further, we have posted close to 1500 openings on oursite till date, acrossdifferent regions in Mumbai and have more than 12,000 candidates registered on our site.
